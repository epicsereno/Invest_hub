# HVAC-as-a-Service (HaaS)

## The Pitch
"We don't sell AC units; we sell 72 degrees."

## Problem
HVAC systems are massive upfront capital expenditures ($5k-$15k) for homeowners. Most units are inefficiently managed and only serviced when they break completely, leading to emergency costs and discomfort.

## Solution
A subscription-based climate model. We install smart, modular HVAC units at $0 upfront cost. We use remote monitoring to optimize energy usage and predict component failure. When a part is nearing its end-of-life, a technician replaces it *before* the customer even knows there was a problem.

## Revenue Model
*   **Subscription:** Tiered pricing based on square footage ($80 - $200/month).
*   **Energy Arbitrage:** We take a 20% cut of the energy savings generated by our AI optimization.
