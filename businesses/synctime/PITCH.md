# SyncTime

## The Pitch
"Timezones are the enemy. SyncTime is the peace treaty."

## Problem
Global teams waste hours manually calculating meeting times and suffer from "Slack Fatigue" as notifications roll in 24/7.

## Solution
A smart scheduling engine that identifies "Golden Windows" (overlapping working hours) for teams. It automatically buffers "deep work" blocks based on the user's local chronotype and manages asynchronous handoffs by summarizing what happened while you were asleep.

## Revenue Model
*   **Pro:** $10/user/month.
*   **Team:** $250/month flat fee for up to 30 members.
