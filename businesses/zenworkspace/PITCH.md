# ZenWorkspace

## The Pitch
"Your desk should care about your health as much as your boss does."

## Problem
Remote workers spend 10+ hours a day in sedentary positions with poor posture, leading to chronic back pain and decreased productivity.

## Solution
IoT-enabled standing desks and chairs equipped with pressure and posture sensors. The workspace subtlely adjusts its height and tilt throughout the day to encourage movement without interrupting the user. It integrates with wellness apps to provide a "Work-Life Health Score."

## Revenue Model
*   **Direct-to-Consumer:** $899+ for hardware.
*   **B2B Wellness:** $15/employee/month for data insights and ergonomic coaching.
