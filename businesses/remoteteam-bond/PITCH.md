# RemoteTeam Bond

## The Pitch
"No more awkward virtual happy hours."

## Problem
Standard "virtual team building" feels forced, cheesy, and wastes billable hours. "Icebreakers" on Zoom are universally loathed.

## Solution
Data-driven, asynchronous team building. We integrate with Slack/Teams to track "collaboration friction." When the AI detects a silo forming, it triggers a "Micro-Mission"—a 5-minute gamified puzzle or collaborative task built into the workflow that actually solves a real, albeit minor, work problem.

## Revenue Model
*   **SaaS:** $4/employee/month.
*   **Custom Missions:** $5,000 for bespoke enterprise culture training modules.
