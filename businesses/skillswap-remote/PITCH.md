# SkillSwap Remote

## The Pitch
"Internal mobility for the remote world."

## Problem
Remote employees often feel siloed and stagnant. Without "water cooler" talk, they don't see what other departments are doing, leading to high turnover as they look for growth elsewhere.

## Solution
A platform for "talent lending" between non-competing companies. For example, a Senior Dev at a Fintech startup can spend a 2-week "sprint" helping a HealthTech company with their security architecture. It provides fresh perspective for the host and professional growth for the visitor.

## Revenue Model
*   **Transaction Fee:** $500 per successful "Swap" match.
*   **Enterprise SaaS:** $2,000/month for internal-only talent mobility within large conglomerates.
