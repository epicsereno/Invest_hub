# SmartPipe Remote Diagnostics

## The Pitch
"The plumber who's always there, but never in your bathroom."

## Problem
Traditional plumbing repairs are expensive due to "truck rolls" (the cost of sending a technician) and diagnostic time. Homeowners often ignore minor leaks until they become catastrophic floods.

## Solution
An IoT suite of non-invasive ultrasonic sensors that clip onto standard home plumbing. These sensors feed data to a "Digital Twin" of the home's water system. Master plumbers can then diagnose issues remotely and guide the homeowner or a junior tech through a 15-minute fix, avoiding a $300 service call.

## Revenue Model
*   **SaaS:** $15/month for active monitoring and leak insurance.
*   **Consultation:** $40 per remote diagnostic session.
*   **Hardware:** $199 starter kit.
