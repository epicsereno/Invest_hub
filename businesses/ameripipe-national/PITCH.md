# AmeriPipe National: The $1 Infrastructure Play

## The Pitch
"Securing 309 million homes for the price of a candy bar."

## Problem
National infrastructure is failing, and the cost of monitoring it is traditionally borne by the government or massive utility monopolies. Homeowners have zero visibility into the "health" of the pipes and wires entering their home until a disaster occurs.

## Solution
A national, low-power mesh network (using LoRaWAN) that provides "Heartbeat" monitoring for every residential water and gas line in America. By partnering with utility providers, we bake a **$1.00 annual "Digital Security Fee"** into the existing utility bill. 

For $1/year, the citizen gets:
1.  **Instant Leak Alerts:** SMS notification if a main break occurs within 1 mile.
2.  **Safety Verification:** A digital certificate of "Infrastructure Health" to lower home insurance premiums (saving them ~$50/year).
3.  **The Infrastructure Portal:** Access to a marketplace of fixes (like our SmartPipe and HaaS partners).

## The Math
*   **Target Population:** 309,000,000+
*   **Annual Fee:** $1.00
*   **Gross Revenue:** $309,000,000/year with virtually zero churn, as it's tied to the utility bill.

## Revenue Model
*   **The "One Dollar" Utility:** $1/year per meter/household.
*   **The Lead-Gen Engine:** We take a 10% commission when a citizen clicks through to hire a remote plumber (SmartPipe) or HVAC tech (HaaS).
