# MicroLease Offices

## The Pitch
"The 'Airbnb' for 1-hour office slots."

## Problem
Co-working spaces are expensive and often located in downtown areas. Remote workers in the suburbs often just need 2 hours of quiet, high-speed internet away from a noisy home environment.

## Solution
A network of soundproof, automated micro-offices (pods) placed in residential neighborhoods, parking lots, and malls. Users unlock them via an app, and the pods feature gigabit internet, 4K monitors, and climate control.

## Revenue Model
*   **Pay-per-hour:** $12/hour.
*   **Monthly Membership:** $99 for 20 hours of pod time.
