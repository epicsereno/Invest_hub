# PrivacyShield

## The Pitch
"Keep your home life private, even on camera."

## Problem
Software-based background blur is glitchy and often reveals messy rooms or family members walking behind the user, causing embarrassment.

## Solution
A hardware-integrated camera module with on-chip AI that performs silhouette extraction at the source. It replaces everything except the user's body with a high-fidelity 3D office or a neutral "professional studio" environment before the video stream even reaches the computer's OS.

## Revenue Model
*   **Hardware:** $249 per unit.
*   **Software Tiers:** $5/month for dynamic premium "environments."
