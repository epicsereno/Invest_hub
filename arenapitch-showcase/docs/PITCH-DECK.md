# ArenaPitch - Shark Tank for Everyone

## Problem

- Inventors struggle to find credible investors.
- Early-stage fundraising is slow, opaque, and relationship gated.
- Private pitch workflows lack a clean access, NDA, and negotiation trail.

## Solution

ArenaPitch gives founders two arenas:

- Public Arena: video pitch discovery, voting, comments, and traction signals.
- Private Arena: invite-only rooms with NDA gates, deck controls, and structured offers.

## Demo Flow

1. Inventor creates a pitch with video, deck, ask, equity, category, and traction.
2. Inventor chooses Public Arena or Private Arena.
3. Investors browse filtered pitch feeds and request private access.
4. Deal room supports offers, counters, accept, withdraw, and history.

## Business Model

- Free public pitches.
- Premium private rooms and analytics.
- Transaction fees on closed deals.
