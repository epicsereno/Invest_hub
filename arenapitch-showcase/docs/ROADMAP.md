# ArenaPitch Roadmap

## Phase 1: Showcase

- Static product demo.
- Public and private arena UI.
- Sample pitch feed and deal board.

## Phase 2: Backend MVP

- NestJS modules for auth, users, pitches, arenas, deals, notifications, and analytics.
- Prisma/PostgreSQL schema.
- CQRS deal negotiation workflows.

## Phase 3: Product MVP

- Auth roles for inventors, investors, and admins.
- Pitch video upload.
- Private room invites and NDA gate.
- Offer, counter-offer, accept, and withdraw flows.

## Phase 4: Live Arena

- Real-time Q&A.
- Investor syndication.
- Deal analytics and portfolio dashboards.
